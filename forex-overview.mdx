## 🌍 What is Forex?

> Forex (Foreign Exchange Market) waa suuqa lagu kala iibsado lacagaha aduunka sida USD, EUR, GBP, iyo JPY.

Forex waa suuqa ugu weyn dunida, waxaana maalin kasta lagu kala iibsadaa trillion dollars.

## Forex Market Participants

Participants Suuqa ugu waaween iyo Role-ka ay suuqa ka ciyaaraan

| Participant | Role |
| --- | --- |
| Banks | Lacagaha ugu badan ayay kala iibsadaan |
| Governments | Siyaasadaha lacagta ayay maamulaan |
| Institutions | Investment iyo hedging |
| Retail Traders | Dadka caadiga ah ee trade gareeya |

## Important Forex Terms

Term-ka ugu muhiimsan ee suuqaan la isticmaalo

| Title | Discription |
| --- | --- |
| Pip | Qiimaha ugu yar ee pair-ku dhaqaaqo. |
| Lot Size | Cabbirka trade-ka. |
| Spread | Farqiga Buy iyo Sell. |
| Leverage | Awood kuu oggolaanaysa inaad xakameyso lacag ka badan capital-kaaga. |

<Danger>
  ⚠️ Forex trading waa high-risk. Ha trade-gareyn lacag aadan awoodin inaad lumiso.
</Danger>
